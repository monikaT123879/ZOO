DETAILS OF THE ADDRESS

Address:

3001 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20008, USA



Landmarks Nearby:

Located in the Woodley Park neighborhood of Washington, D.C. Accessible via Rock Creek Park for scenic views. Close to Woodley Park-Zoo/Adams Morgan Metro Station (Red Line).



Directions by Public Transport:

Metro: The closest metro station is Woodley Park-Zoo/Adams Morgan on the Red Line. From the station, it's about a 10-15 minute walk. Bus: Multiple Metrobus routes serve the area, including the L1 and L2 lines.



Parking Information:

Parking is available at the zoo, but it is limited and costs $30 per car. Consider using public transport, especially on weekends or holidays.



Tips for Visitors:

Admission: Entry to the National Zoo is free as part of the Smithsonian Institution. Hours: The zoo is usually open daily (except on December 25th) from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM, but times can vary.
